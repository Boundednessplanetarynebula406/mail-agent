# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in the `mail-agent` project should be respectful, direct, and useful.

Examples of behavior that help:

- assuming good intent while still being technically rigorous
- giving concrete feedback tied to code, behavior, or documentation
- keeping disagreements focused on decisions and evidence
- being careful with security and privacy concerns

Examples of behavior that do not help:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- publishing someone else's private information
- trolling, dogpiling, or bad-faith review behavior
- posting live credentials, private mailbox contents, or other sensitive data in public threads

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior makes the project worse or less safe for others.

## Reporting

If the issue is conduct-related and not security-sensitive, open an issue asking for moderator attention without reposting the harmful content.

If the issue is tied to privacy or security, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) instead.
